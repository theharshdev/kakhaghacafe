Our Story

A Tale of Two Cuisines

Born from a passion to unite Italy's rustic charm with North India's royal grandeur, La Regale is more than a restaurant — it's a cultural symphony on your plate. Our chefs have trained in the kitchens of Rome and the royal courts of Lucknow, bringing centuries of culinary tradition to every dish.

From hand-rolled pasta infused with saffron to tandoori platters served on artisanal copper, every detail is crafted to transport you to a world where flavor knows no borders.
